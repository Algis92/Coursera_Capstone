Introduction/Business Problem
In this project we will help people who are looking for to buy area for building a house. 
Our analysis should show how much should you invest in area if you want to live 5km, 10km or 15km from city.
How big area for which price client can by.
Should the client to look at areas at south or west from the city center.


Data
The data about area for sale (such as  price, size, street) is collected by scraping local website www.aruodas.lt. 
Coordinates for each area are colected using geolocator and from cordinates we calculate distance from city center. 
Using Foursquare we collect information about closest Venues (supermarkets, restaurants, parks).
